How we ended up being faced with the frustration and regrets of love.

A Family of four is at a nature park to celebrate their daughter's 10th birthday when the 6-year-old son suddenly slumps. At the hospital, the doctor informs the family that he has Sickle Cell Disease. His parents are confused because only one of them is a carrier of the sickle cell gene. The family tries to adapt to their new reality, but his situation worsens, and he suffers a stroke. This puts a strain on their relationship and finances. The silver lining is a bone marrow transplant, but that's not after they find a suitable donor and the funds for the procedure. The transplant seems successful until the boy develops a complication.

STRAIN- Procuder- Toyin Adewunmi, Co-producer- Benjamin Abejide Adeniran, Director- Uduak-obong Patrick, STRAIN is a tense movie which uncovers the pain, blame and regrets that occurs when a wrong decision is made out of love.
